ABOUT

Aspiring programmer (think early programmer) - fascinated by technology - obsessed with design - hi, I'm Tiffany!

In 2014 I graduated summa cum laude from the University of Southern California with a major in Communication and minors in Marketing and Communication Design. After collecting my degrees I hit the road: 4 months touring the electronics markets in Shenzhen, China, 2 months soaking up the sun as a yogi in Ubud, Bali, Indonesia, and 2 months stuffing my face with soup dumplings in Taipei, Taiwan.

Now that I'm back in the US I'm on a mission to be the best front end web developer/designer unicorn that I can be.

In case you wanted to know I'm also an avid reader, adventurous chef, and love it when people reach out to me.

Thanks for stopping by!
